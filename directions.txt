Intro to jQuery

Difficulty Level: Easy

Technologies/skills used: jQuery selectors, animations, DOM manipulation, and traversal



Problem Description:

This is an introduction to jQuery for students who want to get the basics of the library down. Many of our assignments assume at least this level of understanding of jQuery, so this is a highly-recommended assignment for all students.



Requirements:

	Follow the intructions in index.html to learn the basics of jQuery.
